Who am I?

My name is George Clausing. I was born and raised in Marion, Ohio and continue to live here. I currently attend Marion Technical College for a few different programs, and I am hoping to graduate by 2022. Making websites started as a way to pass time, and as I talked to more people about it I started getting requests to make them for people. Once I had done three different sites for people, I decided that I wanted to start making money, and become an offical business, hence the creation of Sindri Websites LLC.

Why the Name Sindri?

Sindri, is the dwarf in Norse mythology who crafted Thor's hammer. With my alma marter being the River Valley Vikings, I wanted to have a Nordic feel to the site and general feel of everything.

Some of My Hobbies:

I love playing table top games, whether it be Warhammer 40k, or Dungeons and Dragons I always have a game that I can go to and enjoy greatly. If I'm not doing that I try to keep myself doing something creative, whether it be painting minis for one of the table top games, or working on the lore for a made up world.
